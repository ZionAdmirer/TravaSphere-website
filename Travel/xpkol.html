Kolkata

Kolkata, the capital city of West Bengal, is a vibrant metropolis known for its rich cultural heritage, artistic flair, and historical significance. Often referred to as the "City of Joy," Kolkata boasts a unique charm with its colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and renowned cultural institutions. Landmarks such as the Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and Dakshineswar Kali Temple reflect the city's glorious past and dynamic present. Kolkata is also a hub for literature, art, and education, home to esteemed institutions like the University of Calcutta and the Indian Museum. The city's vibrant festivals, delectable cuisine, and warm hospitality make it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a blend of tradition and modernity.
