Rome

Rome, the capital city of Italy, is a vibrant blend of ancient history and modernity. Known as the Eternal City, Rome is famous for its iconic landmarks such as the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Vatican City. Visitors can explore the rich history of the Roman Empire through its archaeological sites, museums, and art galleries. Stroll through the charming streets of Trastevere, savor authentic Italian cuisine in local trattorias, and toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain for good luck. With its picturesque piazzas, Renaissance architecture, and bustling markets, Rome offers a captivating experience for travelers from around the world.
